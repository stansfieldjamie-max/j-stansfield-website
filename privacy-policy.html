Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

At Lawn Surgeon, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with UK GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our lawn care services and generate quotes, including:

  • Contact details (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Property details (Address, lawn size, garden condition).
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers like Stripe).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide accurate quotes via our Quote Wizard.
  • Schedule and perform lawn care services.
  • Process payments and deposits.
  • Communicate with you regarding your service or community group status.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in our operations, such as:

  • Jobber: For scheduling and customer relationship management.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing.
  • Formspree: For handling quote submissions.

4. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.
