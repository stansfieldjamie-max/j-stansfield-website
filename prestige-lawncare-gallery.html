A Smarter Way to Save

The Community Rate

While our standard one-off service is £40, the Community Rate rewards you for helping us work more efficiently. It's the perfect way to get a premium service at an exceptional price.

The Deal is Simple:

When you and at least two neighbours book for the same day, the price for a standard maintenance mow starts from just £25 per lawn (for our smallest properties).

Please note: Lawns requiring more intensive work, such as an initial recovery cut on severely overgrown grass, will be quoted individually. They will still receive a discount from the standard one-off rate, ensuring everyone benefits.

It's a win-win: everyone gets a discount, and we save on travel time. Simply mention the "Community Rate" when you get in touch to take advantage of this offer.

The Importance of a Sharp Blade

A blunt mower blade tears and shreds grass, causing stress, browning, and increased risk of disease. In contrast, a sharp, professionally maintained blade provides a clean, surgical cut, allowing the grass to heal quickly and maintain optimal health and colour.
