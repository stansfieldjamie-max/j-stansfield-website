Terms of Service

1. Pet Waste Policy: Ideally, please clear pet waste before our arrival. If waste is present and the removal service was not accepted, we reserve the right to refuse service. A call-out fee may still apply.

2. Access & Gates: Please ensure we have clear access to the garden on your scheduled day. If gates are locked and we cannot enter, a call-out fee may apply.

3. Obstacles & Furniture: Please ensure the lawn is clear of toys, furniture, and debris. While we exercise great care, we cannot be held responsible for damage to items left hidden in long grass.

4. Weather Conditions: We operate in most weather conditions. However, in cases of extreme rain, snow, or frozen ground, we may need to reschedule to protect both your lawn and our equipment.

5. Payment: Payment is due upon completion of the work. For subscription clients, payments are handled via monthly Direct Debit.

6. Cancellations: We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid a late cancellation fee.

7. Booking Deposit: A £15 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking slot. This amount will be deducted from your first invoice. If you cancel, this deposit is forfeited to cover administrative costs.
