The Origin

Lawn Surgeon was born from a simple observation: the gardening industry was stuck in the past. Homeowners were tired of the "man with a van" lottery—unreliable arrival times, vague pricing, and inconsistent results.

We set out to build something different. Not just a gardening service, but a Property Care brand. We combined the reliability of a logistics company with the detailed eye of a craftsman.

Why "Surgeon"?

A surgeon operates with intent, precision, and a focus on health. They don't just "hack" away; they restore.

That is our philosophy for your lawn. Whether it's the laser-straight lines of our mowing, the calculated nutrition of our feeding plans, or the sharp, defined channels of our edging service, everything is done with surgical attention to detail.

A Modern Approach

We believe booking a gardener should be as seamless as ordering a package online. That's why we built our Quote Wizard—giving you instant, transparent pricing based on data, not guesswork.

Our Community Priority system is our commitment to efficiency. By coordinating with neighbours, we reduce travel time and pass those savings back to you, while guaranteeing your street the best service slots. It’s smarter, greener, and better for the community.
